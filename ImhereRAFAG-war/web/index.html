Welecome to the I'm here application

This application allows you to communicate your current location to other people.

It is different from other location apps because allows you to adjust your position and hence to provide your receiver with your precise position.

I'm here is useful when you have to meet someone in large and crowded places.

I'm here' will help you to give appointment and save time and money avoiding long call to describe your position to your friends.

To use the fonctionnality you need :

1 click on the button my position to show your position and generate a tinyurl

- Copy the tiny URL and sent it to a friend

2 you can also find a position of another person

- Click on the button my friend's position

- Paste the tiny URL in the box and next click on the button GO to find your friend's position

- Click on the button itinerary to join your friend

! Use your fingers to zoom in, zoom out or move the map to adjust your position : GPS is not always precise, above all when you are inside buildings.
